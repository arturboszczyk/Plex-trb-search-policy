Privacy Policy for TRBLS
Effective Date:03/10/2025

1. Information We Collect
This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users.
The extension may automatically send error reports to the developer to help debug issues.
No other information is collected, stored, or shared.
Permissions requested by the extension are used solely to enable its core functionality (searching Plex content). No information is shared with third parties.


2. How We Use Information
Error reports are used solely for debugging and improving the extension.
No information is shared with third parties.
No data is used for advertising, tracking, or analytics.
